# Visual & Tonal Theme: The Secret of Moo Yang

## 1. The Aesthetic: "Saturated Tropical Chaos"
* **Palette:** High-contrast neons (Pattaya night signs) mixed with sun-bleached pastels (daytime beach). 
* **Art Style:** "Modern Retro." Thick outlines, expressive character sprites, and static hand-drawn backgrounds with animated foreground elements (e.g., a spinning ceiling fan, a flickering "Open" sign).
* **UI Style:** Industrial-German meets Thai-Street. The inventory should look like a high-tech Stuttgart toolbox, but it’s covered in grease and "I ❤️ Pattaya" stickers.

## 2. The Soundscape
* **Music:** A "lo-fi" blend of Mor Lam (Thai country music) and Kraftwerk-style techno.
* **SFX:** High-quality sizzle sounds for the BBQ, electronic "beep-boops" for the miners, and Hans’s audible sigh of German disappointment.

## 3. The Tone
* **The "Bonkers" Factor:** Lean into the absurdity. The Bitcoin miners shouldn't just be boxes; they should be rigged up to old car batteries and cooled by a literal waterfall of iced tea.
* **Humor:** Fish-out-of-water. Hans treats a roadside pork stall like it’s a Porsche assembly line.
