# The Myth of Chrystal Island: Prologue - "The Stuttgart Sizzler"

## Scene 1: The Last Supper of Efficiency
**Setting:** The balcony of a 5-star luxury hotel in Pattaya. Hans is sitting in a white bathrobe, drinking a sparkling water (carbonated to exactly 3.5 bar).
**Action:** Hans is reading a crinkled, soy-sauce-stained letter.

**The Recap (Hans's Monologue):**
"According to the letter from Uncle Somchai, I am to inherit a 'High-Performance Thermal Energy Enterprise.' He mentions 'integrated hardware' and 'organic supply chains.' It sounds like a data-center-backed green-energy plant. My German heritage demands I bring order to this tropical chaos."

## Scene 2: The Reality Check
**Action:** Hans takes a Thai tuktuk (screaming internally about no safety belt and crazy traffic) to a dusty roadside.
**The Reveal:** He arrives at the "business." It is a wooden shack with a sign that says *“Somchai’s Crypto-Crackle: Pork at 400 Terahashes.”*
**The Interaction:** Uncle Somchai greets him with a Moo-Deng beer at 9:00 AM. 
"Hans! You are here! The miners are cold, the pork is raw, and the electricity bill is due in three hours. Fix the machine, save the family legacy!"

## The Plot Device
The **"Master Private Key"** (the USB stick) is missing. Without it, the "grill" won't boot.
